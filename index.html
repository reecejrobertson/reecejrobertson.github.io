About

I am a PhD student of computer science, with an emphasis in quantum information science, at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County. My advisors are Dr. Samuel Lomonaco and Dr. Sebastian Deffner. I am a member of the Quantum Thermodynamics Group at UMBC. My research interest is quantum algorithm design and quantum complexity theory.
